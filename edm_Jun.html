World's largest modularized ethylene cracking furnace has been successfully delivered from our fabrication yard in Zhoushan, China. The furnace is designed to produce 200,000 tons of ethylene per year, weighing 3,689.6 tons with 53.31 meters in height. It only took 150 days from inception of modularization design to completion of assembly. This is first of the nine sets of cracking furnaces that Wison will fabricate for a 4,000ktpa Integrated Refining and Petrochemical Complex in Zhejiang Province, China.

About Wison Offshore & Marine

Wison Offshore & Marine is a wholly owned subsidiary of the Wison Group. The company specializes in providing innovative infrastructure solutions and quality project delivery services to global energy and marine industry. Our focused product portfolio spans from floating LNG facilities, module fabrication and commissioning, offshore platforms, to high-spec vessels and cranes for customers worldwide. As a global leading LNG solutions provider, we are committed to nurturing development of industry value chain by offering a comprehensive product line. Building upon proven track records, our experienced teams apply their wealth of expertise and leverage two world-class yard facilities in China to deliver integrated EPCIC and fabrication services complying with the highest of international quality and safety standards.
